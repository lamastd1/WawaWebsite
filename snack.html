Ice Cream

Milkshakes

Allergy Warning: product may contain peanuts but ordering a the kiosk it will say if product contains peanuts.

I come to wawa for milkshakes. They are the best milkshakes I have ever had. It isn't too stuck together when you get it so you don't have to wait. Somehow the condensation level is low on the cup which I don't understand. The blend of milk and shake is perfect. I recommened the cookies and cream milkshake you can feel the flavor on your tongue.

Wawa Soft Pretzel

Wrapped in plastic, it looks like something that was bought at a street fair. Do not judge a book by its cover, these are the best soft pretzels I've ever had. The consistency is great. Tastes a lot better with mustard. I highly recommend this item.

Wawa Buffalo Chicken Bites

I am a big fan of anything buffalo. These are ok, there are two issues. One, the dry buffalo is added at the end and you can tell. Some chicken bites taste very hot and others you can't even tell buffalo is on it. Secondly, there is not enough in the bag and you will be left unsatisfied.
